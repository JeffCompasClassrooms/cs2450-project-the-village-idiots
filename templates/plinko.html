Plinko Rules & Tips

Welcome to Plinko! Here is how to play:

  • Click the "Drop" button to release the ball.
  • The ball bounces off pegs and lands in scoring slots.
  • Each slot awards different points based on where the ball lands.
  • You can change how many balls drop at once by clicking the multiplier in the top right.
  • To have the balls drop automatically, click the auto-drop button.
  • ***This game is 100% luck.***

Good luck!
